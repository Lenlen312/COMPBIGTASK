English

Eulogy

A eulogy is a speech or writing in praise of a person or persons or things, especially one who recently died or retired or as a term of endearment. Eulogies may be given as part of funeral services.


Welcome Remarks

To generally welcome all the guests, stating the name of the event and its host and to thank them for coming. to give a brief introduction of the host (the business, organization, family or person) to give a brief introduction of the occasion. to introduce the next speaker if appropriate

Closing Remarks

A concluding remark is the last sentence of the conclusion in an essay. ... As the name suggests, this remark wraps up the entire essay with a period at the end. However, sometimes there could be a question mark or an exclamation mark instead of a period, depending upon the type of remark
